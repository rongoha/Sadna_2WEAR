About Us

Hello!
We are Dayana, Eden and Gal - Information systems students and the creators of the website as part of a project in the WEB programming course at the Tel Aviv-Yaffo Academic College.
The project we chose to create is a site for selling second-hand clothes that will allow private individuals to sell and buy clothes - anytime and anywhere.
The platform will be a kind of intermediary between the buyer and the seller, who prices his items according to his wishes.
The vision behind the project is to change the consumption habits of the community in Israel and around the world: to encourage recycling, repair and reuse over buying new products, to find a warm home for quality items that are not being used and to connect sellers and buyers around the world.
